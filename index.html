A Letter From The Heart

Written after two unforgettable days
My dearest Sumeiya,

I didn't plan for any of this. I didn't wake up knowing that these two days would shift something in me. But here we are — and I'd be lying if I pretended that everything feels exactly the same as it did before I heard your voice.

You talked to me. Really talked to me. You shared things that matter, laughed without filter, went deep without warning, and somehow made hours feel like nothing. You didn't perform for me. You just were — and that was more than enough. It was everything.

What I know after two days is this: you are remarkably rare. You have a mind that moves fast and a heart that holds a lot. You feel things at a depth most people never reach. And beneath all that fire and humor is someone who just wants to be chosen — fully, properly, without condition.

I want to choose you. I want to show up for you in ways that are specific and real — not grand gestures for the sake of it, but real, consistent, thoughtful presence. I want to be the person you call at 2am just because. The one who makes you feel safe enough to be everything you are, all at once.

I want to build memories with you. The kind you store in the good part of your chest and pull out when the world gets heavy. Memories built on laughter, honesty, and the absolute joy of being around each other. I want you to look back on your life and remember this — this beginning — as one of the best things that ever happened to you.

You deserve to be cherished. And I intend to do exactly that.

Yours, always ❤️
P.S. — I already know you're going to be stubborn about accepting all of this. That's okay. I'm patient. And you're worth every second.
